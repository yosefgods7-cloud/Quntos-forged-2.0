# Quant Edge OS
### A Professional Trading Operating System for Capital Growth and Prop Firm Scaling

Quant Edge OS is a high-performance, single-file trading operating system designed for professional traders managing multi-account portfolios. It provides a centralized command center for capital allocation, risk management, and strategic scaling across both live brokerage accounts and proprietary trading firm challenges.

---

## Overview

Quant Edge OS is engineered for traders who treat trading as a business. It bridges the gap between raw execution and long-term capital management by providing a structured environment to track, analyze, and scale trading operations.

The system exists to solve the fragmentation problem in modern trading, where data is often scattered across multiple platforms, brokers, and prop firms. Quant Edge OS consolidates this data into a single, actionable dashboard, enabling data-driven decisions on capital reinvestment and risk exposure.

---

## Core Features

### Portfolio Management
*   **Multi-Account Tracking:** Unified view of live and prop firm accounts.
*   **Equity Curve Analysis:** Real-time visualization of portfolio growth and drawdown.
*   **Asset Allocation:** Detailed breakdown of exposure across different asset classes and strategies.

### Prop Firm Engine
*   **Scaling System:** Automated analysis of payout data to suggest optimal reinvestment paths into new challenges.
*   **Firm Database:** Integrated dataset of top-tier prop firms with account sizes and fee structures.
*   **Challenge Tracking:** Dedicated monitoring of phase objectives and drawdown limits.

### Risk & Psychology System
*   **Psychological Risk Engine:** Monitors consecutive losses and emotion scores to provide real-time risk recommendations.
*   **Drawdown Alerts:** Automated warnings when portfolio or account-specific drawdown thresholds are breached.
*   **Trade Journaling:** Comprehensive logging of pre- and post-trade psychological states.

### Strategy Backtesting
*   **Strategy Lab:** Module for recording and analyzing backtest data to verify edge before live deployment.
*   **Performance Metrics:** Detailed statistics including Win Rate, Profit Factor, Expectancy, and R-Multiple distributions.

### Money Flow Tracking
*   **Capital Allocation:** Track deposits, withdrawals, and internal transfers between accounts.
*   **Payout Management:** Log and analyze the frequency and magnitude of prop firm payouts.

### Offline & Security
*   **PWA Architecture:** Installable as a desktop or mobile app with full offline functionality.
*   **Local Storage:** All data is stored locally on the user's device. No data is transmitted to external servers.
*   **Secure Access:** Integrated login system to protect local data persistence.

---

## System Philosophy

Quant Edge OS is built on four pillars of professional trading:

1.  **Capital Allocation over Random Trading:** Success is defined by how capital is moved between opportunities, not just individual trade execution.
2.  **Risk Discipline:** The system enforces a "risk-first" mindset, prioritizing capital preservation through automated psychological and mathematical guards.
3.  **Data-Driven Decisions:** Every scaling move or strategy adjustment must be backed by historical performance data.
4.  **Compounding Mindset:** The OS is designed to facilitate the transition from a single account to a multi-million dollar diversified portfolio.

---

## How It Works

1.  **Initialize Portfolio:** Add your existing live and prop firm accounts with their current balances and equity.
2.  **Log Activity:** Record trades, including psychological scores and R-multiples, to build a performance baseline.
3.  **Analyze Performance:** Use the AI Insights and Performance Metrics modules to identify strengths and weaknesses in your execution.
4.  **Scale Operations:** Utilize the Prop Scaling Engine to determine when and where to reinvest payouts for maximum growth.
5.  **Manage Flow:** Track the movement of capital from prop firm payouts into live brokerage accounts or personal reserves.

---

## Installation and Usage

Quant Edge OS is a portable, single-file application.

1.  **Download:** Save the `index.html` file to your local machine.
2.  **Launch:** Open the file in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari recommended).
3.  **Deploy (Optional):** The file can be hosted privately via GitHub Pages or any static web hosting service for cross-device access.

---

## Tech Stack

*   **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Tailwind-based utility architecture).
*   **Logic:** Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+).
*   **Persistence:** Browser `localStorage` API.
*   **Icons:** Lucide Icons.
*   **Charts:** Chart.js.
*   **Capabilities:** Progressive Web App (PWA) with Service Workers for offline access.
